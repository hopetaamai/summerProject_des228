A 25-year-old artist from the Bay Area, Hope is a first-generation college student. As the daughter of immigrants who made great sacrifices to provide her with better opportunities, her family's experiences have deeply influenced the way she navigates life. From a young age, Hope has always expressed herself through art. As a self-taught artist, she has evolved from drawing on school desks to developing a distinct visual voice. Her work is deeply inspired by her Samoan heritage, incorporating cultural motifs, patterns, and personal experiences. Upon graduation, Hope aspires to build a career in Art or Creative Direction, collaborating with like-minded creatives to bring innovative ideas to life.

In her free time, Hope enjoys drawing on her iPad, spending time with her sisters, and listening to music. With a Taurus Sun, Capricorn Moon, and Scorpio Rising, Hope approaches her personal and professional life with determination, creativity, and passion.
